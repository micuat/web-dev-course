back to the style (it)

about

Back to the Style, organized by Emozione Danza, was born in 2011 in Porcari in the province of Lucca, as an event to bring together dancers first from Tuscany and then from Italy.

Today the event has a worldwide participation, over 2,000 dancers from all over the hemisphere and more than 3,000 people who come to watch the show; the choreographic competition has over 80 groups and the workshops have been attended by over 400 dancers from Italian and European schools.
