Visited a hobby farm

Also during the Open Farm Days 2024 I had the pleasure of visiting a hobby farm that had lots of cute animals, local food and products for sale and live music!

Lõuka hobby farm

Lõuka hobby farm or Lõuka hobitalu, in estonian, is a farm near Haapsalu, you can find them on Facebook.

My experience

At first we were greeted by a baby pony and it's mom in an enclosure where you could go in and meet them up close! They were both a little stressed from all the people but fortunately they didn't seem that uncomfortable and still came and said hello to the new people.

Next we decided to check out the bunnies who were all huddled together in a group in the shade, since it was a pretty hot day. They were quite lazy but some were curious enough to come and sniff us and eat the carrots we gave them, one even decided to take a nap in my shade!

Would I recommend?

This hobby-farm was one of the best ones I have been to! I saw so many cute animals and I will hopefully have a chance to go back someday:)
