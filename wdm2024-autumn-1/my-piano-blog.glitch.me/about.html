About this Blog

I made this blog to better keep track of the pieces I've played. There have been pieces that I studied weeks or maybe even months for and then completly forgotten the fact I've even played them before.

I will be also giving the piece a rating based on how good the piece sounds, how easy it is, how fun is the practice and how fun is the playing once the piece is fully learned.
