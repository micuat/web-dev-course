Creative Strategy for an Investment & Consulting Brand

Recently, I had the chance to do creative work for a new investment and consulting company — a space that might seem far from fashion or anthropology, but actually isn’t at all.

The challenge was to translate their values — trust, innovation, long-term thinking — into a brand identity that felt bold, modern, and human. I contributed to their visual storytelling, content planning, and brand voice development.

It taught me a lot about versatility, creative strategy, and how to craft narratives that resonate — regardless of the industry. Every brand has a story; I love helping to shape how that story is told.
