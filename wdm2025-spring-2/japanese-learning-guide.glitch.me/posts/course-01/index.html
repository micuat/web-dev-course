Human or animal sounds

    Description
    Giseigo refers to onomatopoeia that imitates actual sounds made by living creatures, such as animals, humans, or other organic sounds. It often conveys the literal sound itself.
    Use
    These onomatopoeia are primarily used to represent the sound an animal or person makes. They can be used literally or metaphorically, such as when someone imitates an animal or describes a situation related to animals.
