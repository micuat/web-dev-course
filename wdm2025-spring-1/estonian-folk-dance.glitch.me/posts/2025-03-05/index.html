Festivals in Viljandi

Viljandi County Dance Festival

The Viljandi County Dance Festival (Viljandi maakonna tantsupidu) is a regional folk dance celebration held in Viljandi County, Estonia. This event follows the same spirit as the national Estonian Dance Festival but is focused on local dancers, traditions, and community participation.

Overview of the Festival

Viljandi County is known for its deep-rooted folk culture, and its dance festival brings together hundreds of dancers from schools, dance groups, and cultural organizations across the region. The event is typically held in Viljandi, a town famous for its connection to Estonian folk music and dance, as well as the annual Viljandi Folk Music Festival.

Night Dance Festival

The Night Dance Festival in Viljandi (Öötantsupidu) is a unique and magical folk dance event that takes place in Viljandi, Estonia. Unlike traditional dance festivals held during the day, this special event happens at night, creating a mystical and enchanting atmosphere under the open sky.

The Role of Viljandi in Estonian Folk Culture

Viljandi is considered the heart of Estonian folk music and dance, home to the Estonian Traditional Music Center and the Viljandi Culture Academy, which trains folk musicians and dancers. The county’s dance festival reflects this rich tradition, keeping folk dance alive at the local level and preparing groups to participate in the national Estonian Dance Festival in Tallinn.
