About the artist

My name is Casey, and I am currently pursuing a degree in psychology at university. Alongside psychology, I have a deep passion for art and storytelling.

I have always loved art and have always believed in the therapeutic power of it.

As a self-taught artist, I primarily focus on semi-abstract art, allowing me to infuse each piece with my emotions and personal experiences.
