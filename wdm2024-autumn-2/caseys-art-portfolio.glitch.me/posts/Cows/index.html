Cows in the field

40x51cm, Acrylic and watercolor on canvas

The story behind the painting

This painting is one of the two paintings I made as christmas gifts for family.

I was inspired by the mugs of the brand Wrendale that I gifted my grandparents the year before and wanted to continue the story of the cows.

Detailed description

Two cows (one highland, one regular) look directly ahead in a pink abstract landscape with pink and purple lupines.
