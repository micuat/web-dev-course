About Leo people

Leo (July 23 - August 22)

Leo is represented by a lion.

Passionate, loyal, and unapologetically dramatic, Leos are the undeniable rulers of the celestial jungle. These fiery spirits thrive on attention, embracing their royal status with a flair for the theatrical and an infectious zest for life. Born to shine, nothing delights a Leo more than being the center of attention, effortlessly commanding the spotlight wherever they go. However, their unwavering self-assurance can sometimes make them oblivious to the emotions of others, as their natural confidence often translates into an unshakable belief that they are always right.
