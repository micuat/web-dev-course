Work vibes

I’ve created several videos that aim to capture and communicate the atmosphere in different work environments. These types of videos are especially useful for people who are job hunting, they give a real sense of what it's like to work in a particular company or office, beyond what a job ad or website can show. It’s all about sharing the authentic vibe, the people, the energy, and the culture.

I’ve produced quite a few of these workplace vibe videos, but one of my personal favorites is the one I made for Rail Baltic Estonia. The atmosphere there was already so positive and dynamic that I didn’t have to 'create' a vibe, I simply had to capture what was genuinely happening. It made the whole filming process feel natural and enjoyable, and the final result really reflected the energy of the place.
