TikTok viral salmon rice bowl

This is a recipe for a delicious salmon rice bowl. I got the original recipe from TikTok but i spiced it up a bit. This dish can be served cold or warm.

-Ingredients

  • Rice of your choice (sushi rice or basic white rice are my faves)
  • Salmon filet
  • Mayo (kewpie is better but regular will work too)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sriracha
  • Sesame seeds
Sides
  • Broccoli
  • Store bought kimchi because I'm lazy:)
  • Seaweed snacks (I prefer wasabi flavour)

This recipe is for the cold version. Fry the salmon fillet, starting with the skin side down. Use high heat so the skin gets crispy. Leave it in a cold place to cool down faster (I put it next to an open window in the winter but be careful for seagulls). Cook the rice according to the instructions. After cooked, i run it through cold water to cool it down.

To assemble the bowl, take off the salmon skin, cop it up to use it as a garnish later. Put the rice in a bowl along with the chilled salmon. Put a healthy amount of mayo and sriracha on top (you can always add more later so don't go overboard) and a dash of soy sauce. Using a fork, mash it all together til it forms a sort of even paste. Leave it to the side while you stam your broccolis. Assemble on a plate with the rice mixture on the bottom, broccolis and kimchi on top. Garnish with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and sriracha on top. Serve with seaweed crisps on the side, so that you can build little bites my assembling bite sized wraps.
