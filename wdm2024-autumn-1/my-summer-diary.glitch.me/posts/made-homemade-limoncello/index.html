Made homemade limoncello

At the beginning of the summer me and my best friend found an easy recipe to make homemade limoncello and decided to try it!

What is limoncello?

Limoncello is an alchoholic beverage usually made with vodka and lemons (usually lemon peel).

How to make limoncello

The recipe I used was from an Estonian youtuber Mirjam Hunt

The recipe

70 cl vodka (preferrably high quality)

10 organic lemons

700 ml water

700 ml sugar

Peel the lemons and add both the peels and the vodka into a sealed jar. Let the jar sit out of direct sunlight for 4-14 days (the longer the better), gently shaking the jar every day.

Pro Tip! Use the peeled lemons to make a delicious homemade lemonade:)

After your preferred time, remove the peels from the mixture, make a sugar syrup by boiling together sugar and water and cooling it down before adding it to your mixture.

NB! Make sure to add the sugar syrup slowly and tasting it to make sure it doesn't become too sweet

And there you go! You just the perfect drink for a dinner with friends or an amazing gift for your loved ones!
