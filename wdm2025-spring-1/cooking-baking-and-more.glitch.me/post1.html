Preparation steps

Step 1: Mix eggs and sugar in a large bowl.

Step 2: Add flour, milk, and oil. Stir well.

Step 3: Grate the orange peel and add to the mix.

Step 4: Pour the batter into a preheated waffle maker.

Step 5: Cook until golden brown. Serve and enjoy!
