My Other Interests

Outside of my projects, I spend time on things that help me unwind, stay active, or just explore ideas in a different way.

I enjoy games like Minecraft and Red Dead Redemption 2 mostly for the open worlds and the freedom to build, explore, or get lost in a story. I also like reading, especially mystery and thriller novels, and I often try new recipes I come across online. Cats are a lifelong favorite, and I make time for fitness and outdoor activities when I can.

These interests aren't part of my technical work, but they influence how I think, create, and approach new challenges.
