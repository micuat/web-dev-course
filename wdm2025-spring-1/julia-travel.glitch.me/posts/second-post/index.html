Milano

Milan, Italy's fashion capital, offers a mesmerizing blend of history, culture, and modern sophistication. Famous for its stunning architecture, Milan is home to the iconic Gothic Cathedral, the Duomo, where visitors can climb to the rooftop for panoramic views of the city. The city's rich art heritage is on display in masterpieces like Leonardo da Vinci’s "The Last Supper," housed at the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie.

The Duomo

The Duomo di Milano is a stunning Gothic cathedral at the heart of Milan, known for its intricate façade, towering spires, and marble exterior. Construction began in 1386 and continued for centuries, resulting in a masterpiece of architecture. Inside, visitors are treated to beautiful stained glass windows and sculptures. The highlight is climbing to the rooftop for breathtaking views of the city and beyond. A symbol of Milan's rich history and culture, the Duomo is a must-see for any visitor.

Gloria Osteria

Gloria, a renowned restaurant in Milan, offers a sophisticated dining experience blending traditional Italian flavors with contemporary flair. Known for its elegant ambiance and attentive service, Gloria stands out with a menu that focuses on high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. The dishes are beautifully presented, often reimagining classic Italian recipes with a modern twist. The restaurant's sleek, stylish interior provides a comfortable yet upscale setting, making it perfect for both intimate dinners and special celebrations. Whether you're indulging in expertly crafted pastas, fresh seafood, or decadent desserts, Gloria promises a memorable culinary journey in the heart of Milan.
