Serenity

40x51cm, Acrylic on canvas

The story behind the painting

This painting, like it's name, represents pure serenity to me. It's the time of year where spring meets summer and nature comes alive, which is why I decided to use both warm and cool tones in the background.

Detailed description

A white and orange koi fish swimming in an abstract background of blue, green and yellow.
