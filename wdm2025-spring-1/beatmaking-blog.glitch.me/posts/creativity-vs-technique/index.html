Creativity vs. Technique

The Battle:

Creativity Technique Raw ideas Mixing/mastering Experimental risks Music theory Emotional impact Technical polish

Pros/Cons:

Too much creativity → Chaotic, unlistenable tracks

Too much technique → Sterile, algorithm-friendly wallpaper music

The Sweet Spot:

Use theory as a toolkit, not a rulebook (e.g., "break" chord progressions intentionally)

Let technical skills enhance — not define — your vision
