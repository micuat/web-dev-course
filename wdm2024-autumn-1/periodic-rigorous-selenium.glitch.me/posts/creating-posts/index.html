Roasted veggies with crispy bacon and tzatziki

I love garlic more than life itself and i always pack my tzatzikis (and all other foods) full of it, I'm talking like 5 cloves per serving.

Recipe

Oven roasted vegetables with bacon crumble

-Ingredients

  • Vegetables of your choice, I used onions, broccoli, zucchini, carrots, sweet potato and red bell peppers
  • spices/herbs of your liking, I used dried rosemary, basil, garlic powder, paprika powder and chili flakes
  • Garlic cloves
  • olive oil, salt and black pepper

Chop up the vegetables so that they are all a similar size. Put them on an baking tray and throw on all the spices as well as some olive oil. Give thm a nice stir and pop them in the oven at 250 degrees for around 30-40 minutes. However, the cooking time depends on how much crisp you want on the veggies. While the vegetables are roasting fry your bacon on high heat until crispy, leave them to cool down on a piece of kitchen roll and crumble when cooled.

Assemble on the plate and add bacon crumble on top and fresh herbs if you want yeaaahhh boy.
