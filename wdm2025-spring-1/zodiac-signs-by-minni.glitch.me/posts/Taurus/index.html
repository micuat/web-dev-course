About Taurus people:

Taurus (April 20-May 20)

Taurus can be described by their representing animal: bull.

Like their celestial spirit animal, Taureans thrive in peaceful, nature-filled environments, where they can indulge in soft melodies, calming aromas, and rich, delectable flavors. Those born under this sign are known for their unwavering reliability, responsibility, and grounded nature. Their loyalty is unmatched, making them steadfast friends and partners. However, their stubborn streak runs deep, and they have a remarkable ability to hold a grudge—so it’s best not to get on their bad side!
