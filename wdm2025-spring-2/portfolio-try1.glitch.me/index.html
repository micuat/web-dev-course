Typing Challenge Web App

A fast-paced typing game that challenges users to type three words as quickly as possible. About Me

Hi! I’m a first-year web design student with a strong interest in both tech and sports. Outside of coding, I enjoy track and field and like trying out different kinds of sports — they keep me energized and motivated.

What I love about web design is the freedom it gives me to express my ideas and explore new ways of thinking. Whether I’m building a game, a blog, or a portfolio, I see each project as a chance to learn, grow, and get creative with code.

Concept

For me, web design is about more than just code — it’s about creating experiences that feel good to use. I try to keep things simple, clean, and fun to interact with. As a student, I’m always experimenting and learning, finding new ways to bring ideas to life through design and development. Every project is a chance to try something new and improve.

Goal

My current goal is to keep building up my skills in web design and development, and hopefully land a junior role or internship where I can learn even more by doing real projects. Long-term, I’d love to become a full-stack developer — someone who understands both design and code and can bring ideas to life from start to finish.
