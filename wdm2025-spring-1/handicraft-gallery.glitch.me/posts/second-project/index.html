Second project

Crocheted top with a flower pattern

I was scrolling on my TikTok for you page as one does and I saw a girl wearing this really cute crocheted top and I immediately wanted to make one myself. I found the pattern online and started making it the summer of 2023 and finished the project a year later. I decided to use two different colors to make it more interesting.

The model on the pictures is one of my close friends from work.
