My Background in Anthropology & History

Studying anthropology and history has given me more than just knowledge — it’s taught me how to see. I’ve learned to analyze systems of power, trace cultural movements, and understand human behavior across time and place.

These tools aren't just academic. They’re the foundation for how I think about the modern world — from global marketing trends to how people express identity through style and media.

Whether I’m working on a research paper or sketching ideas for a campaign, I bring a unique perspective rooted in cultural insight, curiosity, and critical thinking. I believe those skills are powerful in any field — especially ones that thrive on knowing people deeply.
