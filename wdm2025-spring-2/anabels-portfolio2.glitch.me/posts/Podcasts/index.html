My first ever Podcast

My very first podcast is called Inspiratsiooniallikad, which translates to Sources of Inspiration. The podcast explores different professions and the people behind them, what motivates them, what challenges they face, and how they found their path.

I’m proud to say I take on multiple roles in the production: I’m the co-host, videographer, and video editor. It’s been a hands-on learning experience, and I’ve loved every part of the creative process from setting up the cameras to shaping the final cut.

Our most successful episode so far featured dancer Marit Olbri, whose story clearly resonated with viewers. That episode,the third in the series, gained over 1,000 views in less than a week, which was an exciting milestone for a brand-new podcast.

Creating this has given me the chance to combine my passion for storytelling with technical skills, while also learning from inspiring professionals across different fields.
