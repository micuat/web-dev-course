Spent time in Läänemaa

Mari Jürjens concert in Österby

During the summer I also managed to see a concert from one of my favorite singers - Mari Jürjens!

Unfortunately I don't have any pictutes or videos from that evening but I assure you, listening to Mari Jürjens singing with a backdrop of the beach and the sea while the sun was setting and drinking a homemade lemonade was definitely one of the highlights of my summer!

Roosta beach

At the end of summer I spent some time with my grandparents near Haapsalu, where I wanted to enjoy the summer before going back to university.

Roosta beach is a very quiet and private beach with a cool little beach bar-restaurant that serves amazing pizza.

When the weather is good you can also surf there - although it seems that good weather for surfers is means incredibly windy lol.

It was an amazing end to this summer! :)
