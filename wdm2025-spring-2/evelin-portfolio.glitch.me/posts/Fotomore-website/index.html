Fotomore website

I created and designed a portfolio and sales-focused website for a photographer and digital artist. I worked with the client to understand what kind of layout, structure, and features would support her work and audience.

The design included sections for showcasing artwork, sharing background information, and providing options for contact and commissions. I focused on creating a visual style that supported the content without distracting from it, and aimed to keep the navigation simple and clear.

This was one of my first design projects, and it reflects my early approach to user interface design, working with feedback, and building around someone else's creative needs.
