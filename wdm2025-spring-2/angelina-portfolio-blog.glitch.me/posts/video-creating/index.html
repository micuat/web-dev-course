My video creating

To create the video, I had to study the Sony Vegas Pro program, which offers a very large number of opportunities for creating professional videos. Below are two results that I managed to achieve.

First example

In this example, I learned how to edit different parts of a video, connecting them together using smooth transitions. In other words, I selected certain frames from other videos that I liked (in this case, about wolves), in order to later create a beautiful video with calm music. The music is not mine. At the end of the video there are links to the material I used.

Second example

When I visited Finland, I stayed in the city of Porvoo, where I decided to visit various museums. One of the museums was historical, in which I took various photos. After that, I decided to make a slide show using the same program (Sony Vegas Pro) with calm music. The author of the music is Wayne Jones. Here I also learned how to make various smooth transitions between photos.
