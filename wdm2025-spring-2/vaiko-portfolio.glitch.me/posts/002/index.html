Project "Second Base"

This project has been in the works since 2020, when I entered Kadriorg German High School in the Digital Product Design major.

My project was an app for selling used clothes. I worked on this project throughout high school. Initially independently, but it turned into a team project in the last few months of 2023.

I learned a lot throughout the project. Learned how to analyze the market, how to carry out user testing, how to make websites and most importantly - brainstorming ideas to help people or the environment.

This three year project gave me a good base to keep studying in Computer Science.
