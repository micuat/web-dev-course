Hey!
Welcome to my blog, where I share one of my biggest passions – cooking.
Besides making delicious food, I’m also into athletics, which is one of the reasons why high-quality, nutritious meals are so important to me.
Since I have a huuuge sweet tooth, I’m always looking for alternatives to store-bought candy.
For me, cakes and homemade baked goods are a ‘healthier’ substitute for gummy candies and other sweets.
Baking isn’t just a hobby; it’s a way to enjoy treats in a more balanced way while still satisfying my cravings!
I caught the baking bug at a young age, making all kinds of treats with my grandmother—like apple pie, 'kirju koer,' and 'Kass Artur.'
However, I only got into cooking savory dishes later in life.
I started experimenting with different recipes even more after moving out on my own.
