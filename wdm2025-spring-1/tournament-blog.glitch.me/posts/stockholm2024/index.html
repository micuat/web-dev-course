Stockholm 2024

Description

This was my first Overwatch LAN event where I went to as a spectator instead of participating. When Blizzard announced that Overwatch Champions Series 2024 World Finals take place in Stockholm, I was hyped and excited to go there since it was the first time an Overwatch Esports World Finals event of this scale took place in Europe. It was by far the most fun I've ever had at any event. I got to meet and hang out with pretty much everyone I hang out on Discord and see in my competitive ranked games. Going to bars and seeing competitors who used to hate each other with passion squash beef and drink together was definitely a sight to see. To my surprise I got to give out a few autographs to aspiring Overwatch pros
