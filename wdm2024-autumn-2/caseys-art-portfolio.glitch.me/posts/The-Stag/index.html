The Stag

50X50cm, Acrylic on canvas

The story behind the painting

This painting was the first after a long art-block and includes my first semi-abstract background.

To me - the stag represents both pureness and regality and that's what I wanted to express in my painting - a lone stag standing tall and calm on a background of beautiful sunset colors.

Detailed description

The background of the painting is abstract, with pinks, oranges and bronze - like a cloud during sunset.

In the center of the painting, there is a stag, with brown and bronze fur with big antlers, standing and looking into the distance.
