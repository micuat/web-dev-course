Florence

Florence, Italy, is a popular travel destination known for it's rich history, architecture and artistic treasures. It is most commonly known as the birthplace of the Renaissance. In this post I will give recomendations on places to see, where to get the best views to enjoy with great food and drinks.

Uffizi Gallery

The Uffizi Gallery is one of the most famous museums in the world. Housed in a grand 16th century building designed by Giorgio Vasari, the museums holds an unparallelled collrction of Renaissance masterpieces. Here you can see works by the great Botticelli "The Birth of Venus" and "Primavera", as well as artworks by Michelangelo, Leonardo da Vinci, Raphael and Caravaggio.

Palazzo Portinari Salviati

The palazzo is a historic renaissance palace located in the heart of Florence. Built in the 15th century, it was most famously the reidence of Dante Alighiri's muse, Beatrice Portinari. Nowadays it operates as a luxury hotel and restaurant, where you can experiece Florentine history and elegance.
