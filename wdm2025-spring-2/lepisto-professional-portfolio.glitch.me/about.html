About this site

Hi, I’m Julia Lepistö — a passionate student of anthropology and history with a deep curiosity for how culture shapes the world around us. My academic journey has trained me to think critically, research deeply, and understand people in nuanced and meaningful ways. These skills go beyond the classroom — they inform my creative thinking, strategic planning, and vision for the future.

While I love uncovering the past, I’m also looking forward. I’m especially drawn to the dynamic worlds of fashion and beauty marketing, where storytelling, identity, and innovation come together. With a strong foundation in understanding human behavior and social patterns, I’m excited to bring fresh, culturally-aware perspectives to the industry.

I’ve also had the opportunity to contribute to branding and content strategy for an emerging investment and consulting firm, helping shape its creative direction. Whether I’m analyzing 18th-century texts or brainstorming visual campaigns, I bring the same energy: thoughtful, curious, and always people-centered.
