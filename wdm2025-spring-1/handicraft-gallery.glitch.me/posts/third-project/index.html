Third project

A crocheted colorful cardigan inspired by JW Anderson

When the 2020 quaratine hit I was one of the people, who saw everyone on TikTok remaking the JW Anderson cardigan famously worn the singer Harry Styles and wanted to make one myself. So I did ;). The original cardigan is knitted but since I prefer crocheting I did that version. I found inspiration pictures on Pinterest and the patterns I looked up on Youtube.

The model on the pictures is one of my close friends from work.
