擬態語

Conditions or states

    Description
    Gitaigo represents a state or condition of something or someone, rather than a direct sound. These words describe the manner, appearance, or behavior of an object or person in action.
    Use
    Gitaigo can be used to express feelings, movements, and conditions (such as something being tight, uncomfortable, or moving in a particular way), making them useful in both emotional and physical descriptions.
