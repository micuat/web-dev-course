Date played: summer 2019

quick recap

1899, the outlaw era is coming to an end. Van der Linde gangmembers finds theirselves in a mess after failed attempt to rob boat in a Blackwater city AKA Blackwater massacare, even loosing a few of their people along the way.

Arthur Morgan, one of the Dutch Van der Linde's closest people (almost like a son), is trying his best to save gang from breaking up.

The story is hearthbreaking and all characters feel real, especially with a great graphics and detailed animations that Rockstar were able to achive.
