About Gemini people

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Gemini people are represented by twins.

Geminis are spontaneous, playful, and delightfully unpredictable, driven by an insatiable curiosity that keeps them constantly seeking new experiences. Symbolized by the celestial twins, this air sign is drawn to so many interests that one personality simply isn’t enough to contain them. Throw a Gemini to the wolves, and they’ll return leading the pack-thanks to their adaptable nature, they can thrive in any situation. However, their patience wears thin quickly, and once they’ve had enough of someone, second chances are rarely an option.
