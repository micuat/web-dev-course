Chivalry 2 - The Ultimate Medieval Warfare Experience

"For Honor and Glory!" – this battle cry has long been a part of the history of medieval warfare and knightly valor. If you enjoy brutal battles, massive chaotic fights, and strategic moments of desperation, then Chivalry 2 is the game that will immerse you in the heart of the Middle Ages.

As a long-time fan of medieval battle games, Chivalry 2 truly delivers an authentic and emotional medieval combat experience, while also offering plenty of fun and chaotic moments when everything goes completely wild.

This was my first paid game on Steam. I’ve been playing it since 2022, when it was released and I still enjoy it.

Funny moment

My character lost both of their arms during the arena fight and is now running out of blood. It was a definite defeat.
