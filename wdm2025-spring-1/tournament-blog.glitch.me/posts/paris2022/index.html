Les Nouveaux Heros in Paris 2022

Description

This was my first in-person LAN event I attended outside of Estonia. It took place Espot, Paris on the 15th of October in 2022 and it was the first LAN event since the global public release of Overwatch 2 on October 4th, 2022.

Tournament

The tournament itself was played at the Espot venue in Paris throughout a 1-day tournament which included 16 teams.

Most memorable moment

Me and CaptainPrash screaming our lungs off while target calling during Quarterfinals map 2 last overtime teamfight. While he was on the opposing team, their (Frost Tails) PC's were just a few meters away from my team's PC's so we both kept gradually raising our voices until we physically couldn't call any louder. It was truly an adrenaline-filled and exciting experience.

We ended up being teammates in Sheer Cold for the next few upcoming months, competing together in various tournaments including 2022 Open Division Season 2 and Overwatch Contenders 2022 Run It Back
