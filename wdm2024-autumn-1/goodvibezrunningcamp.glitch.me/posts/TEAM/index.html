PHYSIOTHERAPIST

A physiotherapist plays a vital role in our running camp. Their mission is to help participants prevent injuries, improve running technique, and speed up recovery. Every runner's body is unique, and a personalized approach ensures you get the most out of your training experience.

How can a physiotherapist help you?

✅ Injury prevention – By analyzing your running technique and movement patterns, a physiotherapist can reduce the risk of injuries.

✅ Personalized advice – Learn how to strengthen weak areas in your body and improve flexibility for better performance.

✅ Recovery and muscle care – After intense training sessions, the physiotherapist provides recovery support (like massage and stretching) to keep your body in top shape.
