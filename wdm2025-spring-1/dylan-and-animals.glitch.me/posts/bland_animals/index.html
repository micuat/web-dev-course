My favourite land animals🦔

Hedgehog (Erinaceinae)

Wikipedia article

A simple pick, yet hedgehogs are one of my favourite animals. They are cute and they can bee seen quite often in real life. Although the last time I saw a hedgehog was quite sad. It was around six in the morning in the summer. I was walking home and I saw a seagull eating a hedgehog. It scarred me for a day. Anyways, the sound they make is also cute and they make decent pets. They can be quite loving as social as pets.

Fun fact!
Hedgehogs have around 5000-7000 spikes on their back. They can raise and lower them on command. Each spike lasts around a year before dropping out and being replaced.
