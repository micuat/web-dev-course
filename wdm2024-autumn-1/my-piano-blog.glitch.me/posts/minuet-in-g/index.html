Minuet in G

This is in my opinion the best piece for a beginer to start with, easy left hand and easy right hand make it a real focus to get the hands playing together. I studied this pice somewhere between December 2020 and January 2021.

In piano playing I've heard of a saying 1+1=5 meaning that one hand is easy, other hand is easy, but put together its is tricky. This piece is perfect to try to get that under controll.

Background

The Minuet in G is traditionally attributed to Johann Sebastian Bach, but it's now believed to have been composed by Christian Petzold as part of the Notebook for Anna Magdalena Bach in the early 1700s. This piece is a Baroque-era dance, characterized by its elegant, triple meter and simple harmonic structure.
