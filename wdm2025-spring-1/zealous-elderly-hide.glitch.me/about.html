About me

My name is Maria/Mary/Masha, I'm 20 years old Tallinn University Computer science student.

Since early childhood I was interested in video games thanks to my father and older brother. I always watched them play fps games and imagined playing with them while pressing buttons on my disconnected controller.

Many years later I now have a whole list of completed story-games, also not that long time ago I even helped my father get his own gaming pc and installed some games for him. Well how the turntables.

Story-games are helping me forget about all my problems at least for some time and also providing emotions and events, that I would've never expirienced in my entire life. Sounds depressing, but in reality it is not that bad as you might think.
