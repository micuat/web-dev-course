🎮 Gaming Blog

Gaming is a big part of how I interact with and make sense of the world. It influences how I think, relax, and connect with others.

Whenever I have time, I write about the games I play — from fast-paced shooters to story-driven adventures — and reflect on how they shape my mood, mindset, and creativity.

For a uni class called Web development and management, I decided to create a blog where I can share my gaming experiences and thoughts.
