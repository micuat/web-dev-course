"Flow "

"Flow" is a animated film directed by Latvian filmmaker Gints Zilbalodis. This dialogue-free adventure follows a cat navigating a post-apocalyptic world submerged by rising waters.

The cat joins a group of unique animal companions as they set sail on a boat, learning to navigate their differences and fears to survive as a team.

"Flow" was entirely created using Blender, a free and open-source animation software (how cool is that??).

Zilbalodis began production in 2019, dedicating five and a half years to this film.

Flow has won many awards, the most recent one on the 97th Academy Awards on March 2, 2025 for "Best Animated Feature."

The second build

For my second build I decided to build a boat inspired by the movie. Again pinterest was full of cool stuff, like the floating "water lilies" made with trapdoors.

This is exactly why Minecraft Java Edition is my go-to: it offers so much room for creativity! The ability to experiment with builds, customize with resource packs and mods (also better combat).

Building the boat came together surprisingly easily, adding little details like lily pads, lanterns, and soft lighting really made the scene feel alive.
