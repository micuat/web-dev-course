Visited the butterfly house

During the Open Farm Days 2024 me and my family decided to visit Liblikamaja (Butterfly house in English), which is located near PÃ¤rnu.

Butterfly house

The butterfly house is as expected a big house which is split into two - one side has all kinds of butterflies and the other has exotic birds and animals (like sugar gliders).

They write on their website:

"More than 10 species of butterflies from tropical areas all over the world fly free inside our Butterfly house"

My experience

It was quite hot outside the day we decided to visit so it was quite uncomfortable inside the house since it has a tropical climate, and there were a lot of people inside the small house at once so it was quite cramped - it was even hard to get good parking. Aside from that the butterflies were very cool and there were so many of them so it was a good experience.

Do I recommend?

I would definietly recommend to go there but not during an event and maybe not during a very hot day :D
