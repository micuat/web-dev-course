Broadcasts

I’ve also worked on many broadcasts both for clients and for the company I was part of. The most recent one focused on careers in education. It’s called Õpetajaameti mitu tahku, which translates to Many Facets of the Teaching Profession. We interviewed several experts and traveled quite a bit to gather commentary and insights.

Our team built the project from the ground up, and I was involved from start to finish. I created the intro and outro for the first time, and I was solely responsible for filming and editing the final video.

Once again, I have to apologize to my international audience this piece is also in Estonian!
