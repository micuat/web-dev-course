Next Time in New York

16.06.2022 - 03.08.2022

My name is DK, and my trip to New York in the summer of 2022 was a pivotal moment in my life. As part of the NY.SG programme, a group of 12 Singaporeans left for New York for a month to learn how to live and work in the Big Apple. I was one of the 12. The programme has taught me so much and left a great deal of memories for myself. This blog will document some of the cool things I experienced in the city. Click on each image to find out more.
