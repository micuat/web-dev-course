My favourite song

If you could listen to one song, only one song for the rest of your life, what would it be?

It's a really hard question, I could probably make a whole album with songs like that.

If I had to listen to only one song, it'd have to be something that could stand the test of time. Something that never gets old, no matter how many times I hear it. It would need to be a song that evokes a range of emotions, one that could lift me up on bad days and make good days even better. Maybe something nostalgic, or a track with layers of sound and lyrics that keep revealing new depths every time I listen.

For me the song that fills the criteria is Gymnopedie No.1 by Erik Satie. The song is deep and I can find different elements every time I listen. The song is also used as a sample in Minecraft's original soundtrack by C418. Gymnopedie is incredibly nostalgic for anyone who has ever played Minecraft.

Definitely give it a listen, I'm sure you'll love it.
