Visited an alpaca farm

During the summer I had the pleaasure to visit an alpaca farm twice - the first time was in July during the Open Farm Days 2024 and the second was a guided tour we booked with family.

Alpacafarm

Alpakafarm OÜ is located in Pärnu county and is home to around 2000 animals - not only alpacas but also llamas, guanacos etc.

Aside from animals they also have a souvenir shop, where you can find everything alpaca-related from figurines to sweaters to yarn.

My experience

Both times I went to the farm I had an amazing experince - the guides were super cool and all the animals had big pastures and you can tell that they are well taken care of!

We also had the chance to see baby alpacas and llamas, they were so cute and soft!

During the first time I visited I also had the chance to take an alpaca for a short walk, which was a cool and unique experience.

Would I recommend?

Even though it is quite a long drive from Tallinn, I would recommend it to everyone and I definietyly plan on going there next summer as well!
