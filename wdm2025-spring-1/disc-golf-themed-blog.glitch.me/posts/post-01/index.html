Different Discs

For startes, there are different discs for making different throws in disc-golf. There are:

  • Distance Drivers;
  • Fairway Drivers;
  • Mid-range Discs;
  • Putters (Usually different discs for throwing and putting);


That being said, there are also other ways to differenciate between discs such as:

  • Different plastics;
  • Flight patterns (Stable, understable, overstable);
  • Manufacturers;
  • ✨Colors✨;
  • Prints;

and so on.
