Step-by-Step Guide

This tutorial explains how to create banners in Minecraft.

Start by collecting six wool blocks and one stick.

Next, open the crafting table and arrange the materials to create a blank banner.

To add patterns, use a loom and dyes. Special patterns require a crafting recipe.

Using a Loom

To change the pattern of the banner you will have to use a Loom (here's how to create it).

When opening the loom you should see this, on the left there are many patterns which you can use.

However on one single banner you can only apply a maximum of 6 patterns.

Continuing on the right side, there are three spaces for the banner itself, dye, and a pattern one for special patterns.

Special patterns are shown on the right picture. Special patterns can be crafted with paper and various items.

Illagers

Another way to get Banners in Minecraft is by defeating Illagers, the hostile counterparts of Villagers.

Illagers are aggressive toward players and Villagers, so it's wise to bring weapons and armor when encountering them, as they rarely travel alone.

If a player spots an Illager with a Banner on its back, that's an Illager Captain. Defeating it will drop an Ominous Banner.

The Illager Banner is exclusive to Minecraft and cannot be recreated using a Loom. Players can obtain these Banners from Illager Captains or find them in Pillager Outpost Watchtowers.
