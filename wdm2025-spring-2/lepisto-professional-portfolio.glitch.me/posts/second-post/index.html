From the Archive to the Runway — Why I Want to Work in Fashion & Beauty Marketing

People often ask how studying anthropology and history connects to fashion or beauty. But to me, it’s obvious: both are about expression, culture, and identity.

Fashion isn’t just clothing — it’s communication. Beauty trends don’t exist in a vacuum — they reflect history, society, and emotion. My background helps me ask: Where did this come from? What does it mean? Who is it for?

I’m excited to explore fashion and beauty marketing through this lens — blending strategy and creativity with a deep understanding of people and culture. My dream? To create meaningful campaigns that are not only visually striking but also emotionally resonant and culturally relevant.
