Mass Effect 2

Date played: 29.01.2025 - 02.02.2025

quick recap

Shepard is being revived after Normandy explosion with help of Cerberus. After that Illusive man offers him/her to work together to save the Universe. Along the way Shepard meets his/her old and new crewmembers.

first impression

For some reason I'm not a big fan of futuristic setting, it gives me unsettling vibes. This game is no exception, I had a hard time to get used to it surroundings.

aftermath

In short, I'm really dissapointed. Not that long time ago I've finished Dragon Age trilogy and became quite a fan of Bioware, that is why I had high hopes for Mass Effect. Turns out, it has only about 4 main-story missions, beside those, there are only loyalty missions for crew members and side-missions of "bring me that pls" or "kill those guys" sort.
