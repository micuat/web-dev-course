Estonian Folk dance

Estonian folk dance dates back centuries, deeply rooted in the country’s rural traditions and community gatherings. Over time, it has gained popularity, with each generation adding to its richness and diversity. Every county in Estonia holds its own annual dance celebrations, showcasing local traditions, costumes, and unique variations of folk dances. These events bring together dancers of all ages, preserving Estonia’s cultural heritage and strengthening community ties. The most significant celebration is the nationwide Estonian Dance Festival, held every five years, where thousands of dancers perform in unison, creating a breathtaking spectacle of movement and tradition.
