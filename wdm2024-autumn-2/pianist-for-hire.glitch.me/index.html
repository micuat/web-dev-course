Classical Pianist for Hire

I love performing and have previously played at high school graduations, proms, and art gallery exhibitions. On this website, you’ll find details about the pieces in my repertoire.

If you’d like me to perform at your event, feel free to contact me at hugolucapiano@gmail.com. I can play any piece from my repertoire, and if you have specific requests, I’m happy to learn new pieces with enough notice.
