Aftermovies

My favourite aftermovie

I’ve created so many aftermovies over the years. They honestly feel like my babies, so picking a favorite is no easy task. Some took up to half a year to complete due to back-and-forth with the client, and others went through 16 different versions before reaching the final cut.

But I chose this one because it truly captures the atmosphere of the event. I also put extra effort into syncing the visuals with the song lyrics, which wasn’t easy but hey, I pulled it off. The song is in Estonian, so apologies to my international viewers, but I hope the vibe still comes through!
