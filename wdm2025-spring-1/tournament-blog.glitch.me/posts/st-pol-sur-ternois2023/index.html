Saint-Pol-sur-Ternoise 2023

Description

This event was my 2nd time attending a LAN event in France. Due to Saint-Pol-sur-Ternoise being a relatively remote small town in Northern-France it took quite a while to get there. When we arrived to the venue on a beautiful Saturday morning to warm up we found out that the tournament organizers hadn't gotten an actual permission from Blizzard to organize an Overwatch LAN event so the players who tried to launch the game were greeted with a red "Failed to connect to server" because the IP addresses were blacklisted due to too many people trying to log into Overwatch from the same location. It was quite disappointing that the LAN got cancelled because of all the time and money we spent on travelling there. Fortunately, we got to compete under the "Supershy" brand for the tournament so we got our travel expenses covered. The tournament was later played online and we ended up getting a solid 2nd place.
