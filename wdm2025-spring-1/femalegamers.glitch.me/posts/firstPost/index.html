Statistics of female gamers

Dropshipping gaming-related products to female consumers in 2025 appears to be a promising venture, supported by current trends and statistics.

Evidence:

  • Significant Female Gamer Demographics: In Asia, female gamers constitute 37% of the total gaming population, growing at an annual rate of 11%, which is nearly double that of new male gamers.

  • Preference for Mobile Gaming: A substantial 95% of female gamers in Asia engage in mobile gaming, indicating a strong inclination towards this platform.

  • Global Engagement: In the U.S., 69% of consumers play games on mobile devices, with females more likely to engage in mobile gaming compared to males

  • Genre Preferences: Female gamers show a higher preference for casual or mobile games, with 70.1% favoring these genres, compared to 46.5% of males.

  • Market Growth: The global gaming industry is projected to reach $503.14 billion annually by 2025, up from $396 billion in 2023, indicating a rapidly expanding market.

Conclusion:

Given the substantial and growing number of female gamers, particularly in the mobile gaming sector, dropshipping gaming-related products tailored to this demographic in 2025 is a strategic and potentially profitable endeavor.
