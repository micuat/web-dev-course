Camogli

Camogli is a charming fishing village on the Ligurian coast, nestled between steep hills and the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean. Known for its colorful, pastel-colored houses lining the harbor, this picturesque town exudes a laid-back, old-world charm.

The Camogli Promenade

Ristorante Da Paolo

Located by the harbor, this charming eatery offers an authentic Ligurian menu with a focus on fresh seafood and traditional pasta dishes. Enjoy stunning views of the colorful town while savoring specialties like trofie al pesto or seafood risotto.
