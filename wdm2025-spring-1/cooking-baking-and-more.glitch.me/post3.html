Honey-Garlic sauce

You will need:

Preparation steps

Step 1: Cook the chicken in a pan with oil and season it with salt, pepper, pepper flakes and paprika.

Step 2: Then add garlic

Step 3: When the chicken is almost ready, add honey and soy sauce. If the mixture seems too thick, add a bit of water.

Step 4: Once the sauce is boiling, lower the heat a little bit and let it boil for 5-10 minutes
