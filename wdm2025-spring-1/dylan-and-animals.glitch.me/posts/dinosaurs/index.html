My favourite dinosaurs🦕

Bonus category - dinosaurs!!!

Like most small boys, I loved dinosaurs. I had a bunch of dinosaur toys ranging from small figures to bigger fancier toys. Despite the fact I don't play with those anymore, I thought it would be fun to list some of my childhood favourites.

Ankylosaurus

Wikipedia article

My current favourite dinosaur I would say. For starters they just look very cool. I respect, that they are peaceful herbivores, yet they could shatter a T-Rex's bones. I remember having a figure of an ankylosaurus that I quite liked as well.

Fun fact!
Their skin is covered in armour, which is believed to be bulletproof.

Diplodocus

Wikipedia article

The diplodocus was my favourite dinosaur, when I was little. Currently I would say I feel quite indifferent towards them. They are a herbivore and they are very distinguishable by their long tails.

Fun fact!
They were so large they didn't have any natural enemies at the time.

Iguanodon

Wikipedia article

A bit dull dinosaur yet still one of my favourites as a child. I had quite a few figures of them. They were also the main characters in that one Dinosaur movie from the year 2000.

Fun fact!
Iguanodons had five fingers on each hand, just like humans. However, their thumbs were sharp spikes used to fend off predators.
