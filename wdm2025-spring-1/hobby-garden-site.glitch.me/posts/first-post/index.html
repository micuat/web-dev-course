Ducks

Indian Runner Ducks

Currently our family garden contains two white Ducks. They live together with our chickens in our chicken coop.

We used to have 5 ducks who would roam around our garden, eating any pests who would damage plants. They were very beneficial to our garden life but unfortunately they were taken by some foxes.

Although the ducks are domesticated, they are quite scared of humans. Only when raised from the moment they hatch do they get somewhat used to humans being around. But even then, with age, they become somewhat distant.

The ducks lay very big blue eggs, around once every two days. Thanks to their size they taste alot more of egg yolk than normal chicken eggs.
