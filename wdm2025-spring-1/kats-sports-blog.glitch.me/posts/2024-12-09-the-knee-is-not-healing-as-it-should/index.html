The knee is not healing as it should

So, it’s been about 2 weeks since the fall and I feel like the healing process has stagnated. I've experienced pretty nasty falls before and compared to them something feels off. For example, in this spring in one tournament a tackle on me resulted in me falling directly on my knee (on artificial grass this time thankfully) which resulted in my knee swelling up pretty badly, still managed to play in the next three games and score two tries 😝

This is how my knee looked a few days later 🙃

So having experienced that, I started to suspect that something might be wrong. Finally pulled myself together and made an appointment with my team’s physio. After doing some tests she suspected that I have a cyst at the back of my knee and made me contact my family physician so I can get a referral letter to an ultrasound to get it confirmed.
